InkRoot 隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年6月22日；生效日期：2026年6月22日

InkRoot 默认以本地优先方式运行。笔记、图片、标签和设置优先保存在你的设备上。只有当你登录官方服务器、连接自部署 Memos、配置 WebDAV、启用 AI 或主动提交反馈时，相关数据才会按你的操作发送到对应服务。

我们不会出售个人信息，也不会为了广告画像读取或分析你的私人笔记内容。

我们可能处理的信息

  • 账号资料、邮箱、服务器地址和访问令牌，用于登录和同步你选择的 Memos 服务。
  • 笔记、图片、标签、提醒和附件，用于本地记录、同步、备份、导入导出和图片预览。
  • WebDAV 地址、用户名和密码，用于你主动配置的备份和恢复。
  • AI API 地址、模型名、API Key 和待处理文本，用于你主动触发的 AI 功能。
  • 相机、相册、麦克风、语音识别和通知权限，用于拍照、选择或保存图片、语音输入和本地提醒。
  • 设备、应用版本、错误或反馈内容，仅在你主动反馈或启用相应服务时用于排查问题。

官方服务器与自部署服务

使用官方服务器时，账号资料、笔记、附件和同步记录会保存在官方服务器上，用于提供登录、同步、公告和必要的服务维护。使用自部署 Memos、WebDAV 或第三方 AI 服务时，相关数据由你选择的服务处理，InkRoot 无法直接管理这些服务中的账号和数据。

第三方服务

Android 版本可能接入崩溃诊断或统计 SDK，具体以应用设置和构建配置为准。iOS 版本不启用友盟统计；如构建时配置 Sentry 或其他错误上报服务，将仅用于诊断崩溃和稳定性问题。你配置的 Memos、WebDAV、AI 服务、系统分享或图片保存能力，也可能由相应平台或服务处理数据。

本地安全

访问令牌、AI API Key 和 WebDAV 密码会尽量使用系统安全存储保存。普通偏好设置不会保存这些敏感字段。重要笔记建议定期导出或使用 WebDAV 备份。

你的控制权

你可以在应用中导出、删除和迁移笔记，也可以随时关闭同步、AI、通知等功能。你可以在“设置 - 账号与数据删除”中删除本机数据，或通过 账号与数据删除页面 发起官方服务器账号删除。自部署或第三方服务中的数据，需要到对应服务中删除。

联系我们

如对隐私政策或数据删除有疑问，请联系 inkroot2025@gmail.com。

InkRoot Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026; effective: June 22, 2026

InkRoot is local-first by default. Notes, images, tags, and settings are stored on your device first. Relevant data is sent to a service only when you sign in to the official server, connect a self-hosted Memos server, configure WebDAV, enable AI, or submit feedback.

We do not sell personal information or read/analyze private notes for advertising profiles.

Information we may process

  • Account profile, email, server address, and access token for sign-in and sync with the Memos service you choose.
  • Notes, images, tags, reminders, and attachments for local capture, sync, backup, import/export, and image preview.
  • WebDAV address, username, and password for backup and restore you configure.
  • AI API URL, model name, API key, and selected text for AI features you trigger.
  • Camera, photo library, microphone, speech recognition, and notification permissions for photos, image selection/saving, voice input, and local reminders.
  • Device, app version, error, or feedback content only when you submit feedback or enable the related service.

Official and self-hosted services

When you use the official server, account profile, notes, attachments, and sync records are stored on the official server to provide sign-in, sync, notices, and necessary service maintenance. When you use self-hosted Memos, WebDAV, or third-party AI services, the selected service processes the related data, and InkRoot cannot directly manage accounts or data in those services.

Third-party services

The Android version may integrate crash diagnostics or analytics SDKs depending on app settings and build configuration. The iOS version does not enable Umeng analytics. If Sentry or another error reporting service is configured at build time, it is used only for crash and stability diagnostics. Memos, WebDAV, AI services, system sharing, or image saving features you configure may also process data through the corresponding platform or service.

Local security

Access tokens, AI API keys, and WebDAV passwords are stored with platform secure storage when possible. Normal preferences do not store these sensitive fields. Export or back up important notes regularly.

Your controls

You can export, delete, and migrate notes in the app, and may disable sync, AI, notifications, and related features at any time. You can delete local data in Settings > Account and Data Deletion, or request official server account deletion on the Account and Data Deletion page. Data in self-hosted or third-party services must be deleted in those services.

Contact

For privacy or data deletion questions, contact inkroot2025@gmail.com.
