InkRoot 账号与数据删除

最后更新日期：2026年6月22日

本页面用于提交 InkRoot 官方服务器账号及相关数据删除申请。你也可以在应用内进入“设置 - 账号与数据删除”发起同样的流程。

提交删除申请 查看隐私政策

删除范围

  • 官方服务器账号资料，例如用户名、邮箱、头像、登录凭证和账号配置。
  • 官方服务器中与该账号关联的笔记、图片附件、标签、同步记录和反馈记录。
  • 法律法规、安全审计、反滥用或纠纷处理要求保留的信息，可能会按必要期限保留。

不在本页面处理的范围

  • 本机数据：请在 InkRoot 应用内“设置 - 账号与数据删除”删除，或卸载应用前自行清理。
  • 自部署 Memos、WebDAV、AI 服务或其他第三方服务中的数据：请在相应服务中删除。
  • 应用商店订阅、付款或退款：请在 Apple、Google 或对应支付平台处理。

处理流程

  1. 通过上方按钮发送申请，说明账号、邮箱、服务器地址和删除诉求。
  2. 我们会进行身份确认，防止账号被他人恶意删除。
  3. 确认后会处理官方服务器账号及相关数据，并通过你提供的联系方式告知结果。
  4. 通常会在合理时间内完成；如果因安全、法律或技术原因需要更长时间，我们会说明原因。

InkRoot Account and Data Deletion

Last updated: June 22, 2026

Use this page to request deletion of an InkRoot official server account and associated data. You can also start the same process in the app under Settings > Account and Data Deletion.

Submit deletion request Privacy Policy

Deletion scope

  • Official server account profile, such as username, email, avatar, login credentials, and account settings.
  • Notes, image attachments, tags, sync records, and feedback records associated with that account on the official server.
  • Information that must be retained for legal, security, anti-abuse, or dispute handling reasons may be kept for the required period.

Not handled by this page

  • Local data on your device: delete it in InkRoot under Settings > Account and Data Deletion, or remove it before uninstalling.
  • Data in self-hosted Memos, WebDAV, AI, or other third-party services: delete it in those services.
  • Store subscriptions, payments, or refunds: manage them through Apple, Google, or the relevant payment platform.

Process

  1. Send a request using the button above, including your account, email, server address, and deletion request.
  2. We will verify identity to prevent unauthorized deletion.
  3. After confirmation, we will process the official server account and associated data and notify you through the contact information you provide.
  4. Requests are usually completed within a reasonable period. If security, legal, or technical reasons require more time, we will explain why.
